Who am I?

 I am a Master's student in Chemical Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh, PA, graduating in December 2018. I hold a Bachelor of Chemical Engineering (B. Chem. Engg.) degree from Institute of Chemical Technology, India.
I specialize in analytics and data-driven decision-making.

My portfolio


Wait! Chemical Engineering? How did I end up in analytics?

 Owing to my interests in physics and math, I went to Institute of Chemical Technology (ICT), Mumbai, India for Chemical Engineering, one of the best undergraduate programs in the country. The analytically intensive curricula at ICT got me interested in computational analysis. Naturally, applying to Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) was a very obvious decision. The Process Systems Engineering Group at CMU does cutting-edge research in computational process engineering. Besides, the School of Computer Science has been consistently ranked one by USNews. At CMU, I have been working with Prof. Nikolaos Sahinidis in the Department of Chemical Engineering on developing purely data-driven solutions to assist process decisions for an industrial partner.
